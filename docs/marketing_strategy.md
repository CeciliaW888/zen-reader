## üì± Native App Strategy (iOS & Android)

### 1. Branding & Visual Identity
*   **Aesthetics:** Maintain the "Zen" minimalism. Dark mode first, serif typography (Merriweather/Inter), high-contrast accessibility.
*   **Iconography:** The logo should be simple, possibly a book morphing into a digital signal or a quill pen with a play button.
*   **Splash Screen:** (As requested) A breathing/bouncing logo that signifies "processing" or "aliveness" without being chaotic.

### 2. App Store Optimization (ASO) 2026 Trends
*   **Keywords:** "AI Reader", "YouTube Summary", "Ghostwriter", "Narrative Non-fiction", "Productivity Reader".
*   **Screenshots:** Focus on the "Transformation" ‚Äî Show a messy YouTube thumbnail vs. the clean ZenReader chapter.
*   **Preview Video:** A 15-second clip showing the "Paste URL -> Get Book" flow. Crucial for 2026 algorithmic discovery.

### 3. User Acquisition Channels
*   **"ProductivityTok" (TikTok/Shorts):** 30-second clips summarizing popular podcasts using ZenReader. "I didn't have time to listen to Huberman, so I turned it into a book."
*   **Newsletter Sponsorships:** Partner with newsletters like "The Hustle" or "TLDR" which target readers who want density.
*   **Freemium Model:**
    *   *Free:* Text-to-Book, Markdown Reader.
    *   *Pro:* YouTube-to-Book (high compute cost), Cloud Sync, Audio Mode.

### 4. Retention & "Stickiness"
*   **Daily Digest:** A feature to "queue" videos during the day and have them ready as a "book" in the evening.
*   **Streaks:** "Read 5 chapters this week."
*   **Shareable Quotes:** One-tap export of a quote as a beautiful image (Instagram Story ready). This drives viral growth.
